Who We Are

Skyborne Studios is a two person independent team dedicated to crafting visually striking games enriched by immersive soundtracks. We channel our creativity through the interactive medium to deliver unforgettable, player first experiences.

Skyborne Chronicles is our debut project under the Skyborne Studios banner. We're excited to share our vision and showcase our approach to game development. We hope you'll share our excitement and appreciation for Skyborne Chronicles as we continue to learn, improve, and raise the bar with future projects.
