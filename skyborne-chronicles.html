Skyborne Chronicles

A city-building, resource management survival game set in an alternate World War I timeline. Your mission: manage a flying city, keep it airborne and ensure humanity's survival for 100 years.

Steam Page Watch Trailer
The Story

Survive Above the Clouds

Nuclear weapons ended the trench warfare of WWI — but they also rendered the surface unlivable. Humanity's last hope floats above the ruins: sky cities held aloft by a mysterious resource called Aerium.

Lead your people, manage vital resources, construct buildings, and fend off hostile cities. Every decision matters — let the city fall below altitude, and it's over.

Genre City Builder / Survival / Resource Management
Setting Alternate WWI — Steampunk
Inspiration Frostpunk, Mortal Engines

Gameplay

Core Features

City Building

Construct buildings to grow your population and produce Aerium to keep your city airborne.

Battle Sequences

Defend your city from hostile forces in real-time combat mini-games.

Citizen Events

React to epidemics, riots, and crises. Poor decisions cost altitude — and lives.

Interior Inspection

Dive inside your city to inspect buildings and manage your people up close.
